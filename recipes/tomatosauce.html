Tomatoe Sauce

Description

There's nothing like homemade tomato sauce to put garden-fresh tomatoes to good use. This tomato sauce recipe slowly simmers on the stove for hours, creating layers of flavor that are impossible to resist.

    Ingredients
  • 10 very ripe tomatos
  • Butter and olive oil
  • onionn, bell pepper, carrots celery stalks
  • red wine
  • garlic, basil, italian seasoning, bay leaf
  • tomato paste
    Steps:
  1. boil the tomatos until the slkin is peeling and then put them in an ice bath
  2. peel the skins and pull out the seeds
  3. chop eight tomatos and puree them in a blender, chop remaining tomatos and put them aside
  4. saute the veggies and garlic until tender. add the pureed and chopped tomatos
  5. stir in the wine, basil, and seasoning. Add celery stalks and bay leaf
  6. simmer for two hours, stir in the tomato paste, then continue simmering for two more hours
  7. remove the bay leaf and celery before serving
